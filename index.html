Our Roots

Baked with Humility, Grounded in Community

Founded in 2018, The Daily Crumb started as a small passion project in a home kitchen with a single oven and passion. Today, we supply our customers with clean, wholesome, completely preservative-free baked goods.

We partner exclusively with local millers and local dairy farms. By retaining the natural grain bran and avoiding speed-additives, every item on our counter supports local farming while nourishing your family.

"We do not cut corners. If a single batch doesn't meet our standards by dawn, it doesn't cross our counter." — Founder & Head Baker
