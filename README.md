# Local Business Website

A professional and responsive local business website developed to help small businesses improve their online presence, attract more customers, and build trust online.

This project was designed for a local bakery business and focuses on providing a clean modern user experience with responsive design and customer-friendly navigation.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The website helps the business:

* Look more professional online
* Showcase products and services
* Increase customer trust
* Improve online visibility
* Make it easier for customers to contact the business

---

## ✨ Features

Interactive resume & portfolio sections
Responsive design (works on mobile & desktop)
Contact form with email notifications
SEO-friendly structure for better visibility on search engines
A professional homepage introducing who you are
A projects section showcasing your work
An about / resume section highlighting your skills
A contact section where people can reach you
